Commercial Title Services

Comprehensive solutions for complex commercial transactions.

Navigating commercial real estate transactions requires specialized knowledge and precision. Danube Title Agency provides expert title and escrow services tailored for developers, investors, and corporate entities.

  • Multi-site and multi-state portfolio transactions
  • Complex zoning and land use endorsements
  • Mezzanine financing and entity transfers
  • Expert clearing of intricate title defects

Residential Title Services

Securing your dream home with absolute certainty.

Whether you are buying your first home or refinancing your current one, our team ensures a smooth, timely, and secure closing process.

  • Thorough title searches and examinations
  • Title insurance policies (Owner's and Lender's)
  • Escrow and settlement services
  • Clear communication out to all parties

Loan Modification

Efficient processing for loan adjustments.

We support lenders and borrowers through the loan modification process by providing necessary title updates and ensuring the modification's priority position.

  • Modification Guarantees and Endorsements
  • Bring-down searches
  • Recording services for modification agreements
  • Fast turnaround times for high-volume requests

Construction Draws

Risk mitigation for construction lending.

We provide specialized services for construction loans, ensuring that funds are disbursed safely and your lien position remains secure throughout the project.

  • Date-down endorsements before each draw
  • Mechanic's lien waiver tracking and collection
  • Review of sworn statements and architectural certificates
  • On-site inspections coordination
